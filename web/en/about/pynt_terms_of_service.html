PYNT Terms of Use

DuyBeo-HUB Ltd (hereafter "DuyBeo-HUB") is the owner and operator of the Facility "PYNT" (hereafter “Facility") and the services provided there ("Services"). The following describes the rights and obligations between DuyBeo-HUB and Users with respect to PYNT use.

Article 1 - Purpose of Use
The purpose of the Facility is to enable the "gathering, thinking, and understanding of subject matter in a non-discriminatory manner regarding status, industry, title, or position" and “to solve social issues by continuing to work together with colleagues with a fresh perspective.”

Article 2 - Users
The following persons are eligible to use the Facility (hereinafter "User/Users"):
(1) A member who has become an individual member of the Facility, in accordance with the Membership Agreement (hereinafter an "Individual Member").
(2) A member who has become a Corporate Member of the Facility, in accordance with the Membership Agreement (hereinafter "Corporate Member"; together with Individual Members, "Members, etc."). A person designated by the Corporate Member as a User (hereinafter a "Corporate Member User") from among the officers, employees, and other Members of the Facility (hereinafter "Members, etc.") who are full-time employees of the Facility (hereinafter "Corporate Members"). However, the number of Corporate Member Users is limited to the number specified in the Membership Agreement.
(3) Persons authorized by DuyBeo-HUB to accompany an Individual Member or Corporate Member (hereinafter a "Guest"). However, the number of Guests who may accompany an Individual Member or a Corporate Member is limited to the number specified in the Membership Agreement.
(4) Any other person who is permitted to use the Facility by DuyBeo-HUB, including the management and operation of the Facility, as entrusted by the company (hereinafter the same).

Article 3 – Service Contents
The contents of this service are as follows.
(1) Use of meeting rooms.
(2) Use of equipment and tools.
(3) Use of the Facility and equipment for events, etc. conducted by Members in the Facility.
*(4) Other services as DuyBeo-HUB may provide from time to time.

Article 4 - Available Hours of Use
The Facility’s available hours of use shall be determined separately.

Article 5 - Usage Fees, etc.
DuyBeo-HUB shall not charge any usage fees, admission fees, or other fees for using the Facility, except for those stipulated in this Agreement and the Membership Agreement. Users who participate in events or services as specified in Article 3, Item 3 and 4, that require participation fees, etc., may bear those costs separately, to the event organizer, etc.

Article 6 - Compliance
In using the Facilities, Users shall agree in advance to, and comply with the following items.
(1) Act in a cooperative manner with other Users.
(2) Comply with the descriptions in the Facility use manual, etc. when using the Facility and equipment.
(3) Comply with the Terms of Use, related rules and regulations, and Facility employee instructions.

Article 7 – Equipment Usage
The User must adhere to the conditions, procedures, etc. outlined in the Facility Use Manual when utilizing equipment.

Article 8 - About the Strap with Card Case and Face Authentication System
Guests visiting the Facility may borrow a strap with a card case (hereinafter "Strap") only when they consent to the Terms of Use of the Facility.
Members, etc. who use the Facility may borrow a Members-only Strap when they complete the registration with the face authentication system when registering as a member and use the system prior to using the Facility.
Users who receive the strap shall agree to and comply with the following conditions in advance.
(1) The User does not lend nor give the strap to another person.
(2) In the event of loss, the User immediately notifies DuyBeo-HUB.
(3) In case of the loss or damage to the strap, the User pays the cost.

Article 9 - Carrying the Strap at All Times
When staying at the Facility, guests shall carry the strap at all times so that it can be readily verified. After using the Facility, the strap must be returned to the reception desk on the second floor or to the designated location.

Article 10 - Events, etc.
1. The User agrees that events, etc. organized by Members and held in the Facility will be carried out with the prior consent of DuyBeo-HUB. Events will be held in a portion of the Facility.
2. DuyBeo-HUB will notify members as early as possible of the status of the event.
3. If a Member wishes to hold an event in the Facility, the Member shall consult with DuyBeo-HUB in advance regarding its details. If the company deems that the event is in line with the Facility’s purpose, the Member may use the portion of the Facility that DuyBeo-HUB has approved for use. Actual Facility use shall be conducted in accordance with the terms and conditions of use stipulated by DuyBeo-HUB.
4. In the event that the company requests cooperation for the purpose of revitalizing the Facility or promoting mutual friendship among Members, Members are requested to cooperate to the extent possible in such events, as stipulated in this Article.
5. For the purpose of revitalizing the Facility and promoting friendship among Members, Members are requested to fully understand that the Facility is designed to induce collaboration among Members and to promote business co-creation, and to contribute to the development of the Facility. Accordingly, Members shall cooperate as much as possible.

Article 11 - Confidentiality
Users shall not disclose, divulge, or pass on to any third party any business, technical, or other confidential information (hereinafter "Confidential Information") of DuyBeo-HUB, to Corporate Members, other Users, or third parties that the User has come to know in the course of using the Facility or Services. However, the following information shall not be considered Confidential Information.
(1) Information that is already public knowledge at the time the information is learned.
(2) Information that was already legally in the possession of the User at the time he/she became aware of it.
(3) Information that becomes public knowledge through no fault of the User after he/she becomes aware of it.

Article 12 - Garbage Disposal
1. The User shall keep the space he/she uses clean at all times.
2. The User shall dispose of garbage in the garbage bins provided in the Facility, in accordance with the Facility Use Manual, etc.

Article 13 - Handling of Lost Items
In principle, items lost or abandoned at the Facility will be stored on site. If the owner does not come to claim it after a predetermined time period, the property will be disposed of appropriately.

Article 14 - Security Cameras
1. The User agrees in advance to the following matters concerning security cameras:
(1) Cameras are installed in the Facility for security purposes.
(2) Images captured by security cameras (hereinafter "captured data") shall be stored by DuyBeo-HUB or its subcontractor for a certain period of time.
2. DuyBeo-HUB may use filming data for the purpose of confirming Facility operations, checking for violations of these rules and regulations, incidents of theft, fire, lost property, and for cooperating with police and other authorities in criminal investigations.

Article 15 - Facility Closure
1. DuyBeo-HUB may suspend use of all or part of the Facility (hereinafter "Closing") even during the Facility's available hours if any of the following events occurs. In such cases, Users may not be able to use all or part of the Facility.
(1) When equipment cannot be used due to malfunction, damage, maintenance, etc.
(2) When equipment cannot be used due to the inability of the Facility's employees to respond to requests.
(3) When the Facility is subject to periodic inspections, etc. as required by law.
(4) When DuyBeo-HUB determines that the Facility cannot be used safely by the Users due to weather conditions or disasters.
(5) When the use of the Facility is restricted due to administrative guidance, legal stipulations, or other reasons.
(6) In the event of renovation, equipment modification or repair, or other construction work being conducted at the Facility or its premises.
(7) When DuyBeo-HUB deems it impossible to conduct business due to an event, etc. being held on the Facility's premises.
(8) When DuyBeo-HUB occupies the Facility for its own events, etc.
(9) When DuyBeo-HUB deems it necessary to do so.
2. In principle, DuyBeo-HUB will notify Members at least one day in advance if the Facility is scheduled to be closed. However, in case of emergency, the company is not required to give prior notice.

Article 16 - Facility Shutdown
DuyBeo-HUB reserves the right to close all or part of the Facility and suspend the Service in the following situations.
(1) When DuyBeo-HUB determines that using the Facility has become difficult due to weather, disaster, etc., and that use cannot be resumed for a considerable period of time.
(2) DuyBeo-HUB deems it difficult to continue operation of the Facility from a managerial standpoint.

Article 17 - Prohibited Acts
The following acts by Users are prohibited in the Facility and its vicinity.
(1) Entering the Facility outside available hours.
(2) Use of the Facility after 7:00pm for persons under 16 years of age or after 10:00pm for persons under 18 years of age.
(3) Entering off-limits areas determined by DuyBeo-HUB.
(4) Damage, destruction, or theft of Facility equipment, appliances, fixtures, or other items under Facility control.
(5) Placing billboards, posters, or other advertising materials inside or outside the Facility without permission.
(6) Smoking inside the Facility.
(7) Disposing of cigarette butts, paper waste, dust, or other refuse, or leaving refuse in violation of this Facility use manual.
(8) Duplicating the strap to enter the Facility.
(9) Taking photographs inside the Facility without DuyBeo-HUB's permission.
(10) Making printed materials, including business cards, using the address and name of the Facility or posting them on a website or other electronic media without DuyBeo-HUB's permission.
(11) Using confidential information without permission, or disclosing or leaking such information to a third party.
(12) Keeping animals in the Facility or bringing animals into the Facility (excluding guide dogs, hearing dogs, service dogs, etc. that have been permitted by DuyBeo-HUB).
(13) Selling goods or providing services or conducting or soliciting business in the Facility without DuyBeo-HUB's permission.
(14) Solicitation (including solicitation for group memberships), political activities, signature activities, or religious activities (except for individual religious activities such as individual worship services), whether for profit or non-profit (excluding activities that are in accordance with the purpose of the Facility).
(15) Selling, soliciting, mediating, or engaging in any other sales activities not desired by Members, such as network businesses, MLM (multi-level marketing), pyramid schemes, insurance, information materials, etc.
(16) Disturbing other Users or interfering with the use of the Facility.
(17) Acts of obstructing or disturbing the business of employees of the Facility, such as holding up Facility staff through interviews, telephone calls, or other methods, without just cause.
(18) Disturbing the order of the Facility.
(19) Slandering or defaming other Users, Facility's employees, the Facility itself, or DuyBeo-HUB.
(20) Violent or threatening behavior toward other Users or Facility staff.
(21) Stalking other Users or employees of the Facility, such as ambushing, following, or persistently talking to other Users or Facility staff.
(22) Acts offensive to public order and morals, such as molestation, peeping, exposure, etc.
(23) Transferring or receiving illegal items such as drugs or furniture in the Facility.
(24) Bringing hazardous materials (explosives, explosive substances, or other items deemed dangerous by DuyBeo-HUB) into the Facility.
(25) Violating any laws or regulations in the Facility.
(26) Transmitting antisocial, violent, or bizarre information.
(27) Any other acts DuyBeo-HUB deems inappropriate.

Article 18 - Refusal of Entry/Exit, and Restrictions on Facility Use
DuyBeo-HUB may order the User to be prohibited from entering, leaving, or otherwise restricted from using the Facilities for any of the following reasons. Users shall immediately comply with such orders.
(1) When DuyBeo-HUB deems it necessary to restrict the use of the Facilities due to congestion or other reasons within the Facilities.
(2) In the event that the User does not comply with the Terms of Use, related rules and regulations, and the Facility usage manual.
(3) In the event that the User does not follow the instructions of Facility employees.
(4) When DuyBeo-HUB or the Facility Operation Team deems that the User will cause inconvenience to other Facility Users.
(5) If the User has been expelled from the Facility in the past (including cases where the User committed an act that resulted in expulsion, and as a result voluntarily withdrew from Membership).
(6) When a User has committed an act that falls under the prohibited acts stipulated in the preceding Article.
(7) When a User falls under the category of antisocial forces (as defined in Article 21, Section 1).
(8) In any other cases where DuyBeo-HUB deems the use of the Facility to be inappropriate.

Article 19 - Compensation for Damages
1. In the event that a Member, etc. intentionally or negligently causes damage to the Facility or items incidental to it, DuyBeo-HUB, other Users, third parties, the Member shall promptly notify DuyBeo-HUB.
2. If the User causes damage to DuyBeo-HUB, the User shall immediately compensate DuyBeo-HUB for such damage at the User's responsibility and expense, in accordance with the company’s demand.
3. If the User causes damage to any person other than DuyBeo-HUB, the User shall deal with such damage in a sincere manner, settle the matter on his/her own responsibility, and shall not cause any trouble or damage to DuyBeo-HUB.

Article 20 - Disclaimer
DuyBeo-HUB shall not be liable for any damages incurred by the User due to any of the following reasons, except in the case of intentional or negligent acts. However, if DuyBeo-HUB is negligent (excluding gross negligence), it shall be liable only for ordinary and direct damages actually incurred by the User.
(1) Damages caused by natural disasters such as earthquakes and floods; the spread of infectious diseases; fire; transportation disruption; rioters or theft; malfunction, damage or IT breakdown; infrastructure and other communication equipment; and various other facilities and equipment; and accidents (including loss, damage, or data leaks and resulting direct or indirect damages).
(2) Damage incurred by Users of the Facility or other third parties.
(3) Damage caused by deviation from the proper use of the Facility as described in the Facility Use Manual.
(4) Damage caused by maintenance work, repairs, alterations, etc. performed for the upkeep and preservation of the Facility's construction and equipment, etc.

Article 21 - Exclusion of Anti-social Forces, etc.
1. The User shall manifest and vouch for the following to DuyBeo-HUB.
(1) The User does not fall under any of the following categories: organized crime groups, companies affiliated with organized crime groups, general assemblymen, or similar persons or their Members (hereinafter, collectively "anti-social forces"), and will not fall under any of the above categories in the future.
(2) The applicant does not allow anti-social forces to use its own name to enter into a contract of use.
2. In addition to the preceding paragraph, the User manifests and vouches that he/she does engage in any of the following acts, directly or indirectly, and will not engage in any such acts in the future.
(1) Use of the Facility as an office or other base of operations for anti-social forces.
(2) Using oneself or a third party to engage in fraudulent or violent acts, threatening language, or unreasonable demands beyond the scope of legal responsibility.
(3) Any act that obstructs DuyBeo-HUB's business or damage DuyBeo-HUB's credibility by using deception or force.
(4) Introducing capital or funds or establishing a relationship with anti-social forces, regardless of the name of the relationship.
(5) Providing funds to anti-social forces in any name.
(6) The involvement of anti-social forces in the Member's business.

1. DuyBeo-HUB obtains and uses the name, zip code, address, location, telephone number, e-mail address, and other information of Members and others in order to facilitate the smooth operation of the Facility. DuyBeo-HUB appropriately and strictly manages personal information obtained from Users when they register as Members, use the Facility, or participate in events, in accordance with the company’s Privacy Policy. DuyBeo-HUB may disclose information such as applications for events and use of the Facility only upon request from the Member himself/herself or a Corporate Member of the same corporation.
