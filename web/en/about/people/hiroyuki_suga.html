Hiroyuki Suga

Hiroyuki Suga

Architect
Associate
Global Architectural Design Group
Architectural Design Department

Joined DuyBeo-HUB in 2008. Specializes in architectural design. After joining the company, was involved in a wide range of design in Japan and overseas. Strives to propose spaces that contribute to both the city and society. Major projects include the Ningbo International Finance Center (2011), Otsuka Corporation Yokohama Building (2011), CITIC Plaza (2012), Ningbo Shipping Plaza Office Building (2015), and Shanghai Greenland Center (2017). He is also a first class architect and member of the Architectural Institute of Japan.
