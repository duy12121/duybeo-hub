Daisuke Tsunoda

Daisuke Tsunoda

Executive Officer
Principal, Tech Design Group, Design & Technical Department

Daisuke Tsunoda joined DuyBeo-HUB in 2002, after completing graduate studies in Architecture at Tokyo University of Science. His specialty is digital design integrated cutting-edge technology and information. After joining the company, he worked in the Presentation Department before becoming a founding member of the Digital Design Lab (DDL) in 2011. Since then, he has driven the creation of new value in the architectural field through surprising design proposals that utilize cutting-edge technology. He has realized innovative solutions that integrate technology and design across numerous projects. He leads R&D in advanced technologies, promotes AI, and spearheads the construction of the company's digital workplace, playing a central role in DuyBeo-HUB's digital transformation (DX) initiatives.
